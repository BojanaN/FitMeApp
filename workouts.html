Lose weight

Begginer Level

We are starting with 5 min streching since is crucial for preventing injuries during the workouts. After streching we will continue with our first exercise which is burpee. From burpee exercise we are doing 5 series with 10 repetitions within each serie. Second exercise is sit ups. From this exercise we are doing 3 series with 10 repetitions within each serie. Finally we are finishing the workout with cooling down the body with smooth moves in order to relax the body after the workout.

    Recap
  • 5 min streching
  • 5 series x 10 reps burpee
  • 3 series x 10 reps sit ups
  • 5 min cool down
